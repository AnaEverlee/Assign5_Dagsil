👋 Hi, I'm Leanne!

I am a first year college student of De La Salle University Dasmarinas, undertaking Bachelor of Science in Information Technology as my undergraduate course. I aspire to become a UI/UX Designer equipped with front-end skills, as well as pursue my hobby of writing as a sideline. It's an ambitious dream--but that's me.

Can you guess where I am? 🤔

That's right! Singapore and the infamous merlion. This is the country where I spent most of my childhood. Despite staying in the Philippines for the meantime to pursue my college degree, I still go back to Singapore every now and then since it is where my parents work.
Let me take you on a virtual tour around the small country through this video from YouTube.

Moving forward... What would I be if not a UI/UX Designer? 💻

Initially, I wanted to become a model.

As a child, I've always dreamt of becoming a model. But painful realizations occured to me while growing up (for instance, my physical appearance does not fit the beauty standards in the modelling industry). Although it was sad bidding farewell to my childhood dream, I still love posing for the camera and playing dress as it boosts my self-confidence and self-esteem.

Then, I began developing a love for writing.

Another hobby of mine is writing. Currently, I'm in the middle of a book project which I will release around 2023. I know it won't be perfect since it would be my first ever published book, but I hope the readers who lay their hands on it would appreciate the story and the characters.
